---
title: Avoid ambiguity
description: Clarity is key.
---

- Declare what you mean, be bold with your proposals. If you don't present your ideas, then you won't be able to gain the necessary feedback that makes things stronger. [[Zettle/Feedback Loops are the key to motivation and a successful learning process]] and not being vague allows for them to occur. [[Zettle/Sharing your work with others leads to better results]].
